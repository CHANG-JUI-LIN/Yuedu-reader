# 隱私權政策

閱讀（Yuedu Reader）
最後更新：2026 年 6 月 30 日

[English](#en)

閱讀是一款以本機為主的 iOS 閱讀器。我們不經營分析、追蹤或廣告伺服器，也不會出售你的個人資料。你的書籍、閱讀進度、書籤、標註、RSS、書源與閱讀偏好預設儲存在你的裝置上。

## 我們不收集的資料

- 沒有第三方分析、行為追蹤或廣告 SDK。
- 沒有由開發者經營的伺服器記錄你的閱讀行為。
- 我們不會為廣告目的出售、出租或分享你的資料。

## 本機資料

你匯入的 EPUB、TXT、漫畫、音訊檔案，閱讀進度、書籤、標註、書架、RSS 訂閱、書源、替換規則與偏好設定主要保存在本機。你可以透過 iOS 或 App 內功能刪除相關資料。

## 選用同步與登入

如果你開啟 iCloud、WebDAV 或其他同步功能，資料會傳送到你選擇的服務。iCloud 由 Apple 提供；WebDAV 或其他服務由你自行設定。我們不經營這些服務，也不會收到這些同步內容。

如果你使用 Apple 或 Google 登入，App 只會在本機保存顯示名稱、電子郵件與帳號識別碼等基本資訊，用於識別你的帳號與同步狀態。登入不是使用閱讀功能的必要條件。

## 你自行設定的網路來源

閱讀可以連線到你提供的 RSS、書源、線上閱讀、TTS 或 WebDAV 位址。這些請求會送到你選擇的第三方服務；第三方服務如何處理資料，適用其自身政策。請不要把敏感內容送往你不信任的來源。

## 兒童隱私

本 App 並非針對 13 歲以下兒童設計。我們不會在知情情況下收集兒童個人資料。

## 政策變更

若本政策更新，我們會更新本頁的「最後更新」日期。重大變更會在 App 或專案頁面上提示。

## English {#en}

# Privacy Policy

Yuedu Reader
Last updated: June 30, 2026

Yuedu Reader is a local-first iOS reading app. We do not operate analytics, tracking, or advertising servers, and we do not sell your personal data. Your books, reading progress, bookmarks, annotations, RSS feeds, book sources, and reading preferences stay on your device by default.

## What We Do Not Collect

- No third-party analytics, behavioral tracking, or advertising SDKs.
- No developer-operated server logs your reading activity.
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data for advertising.

## Local Data

Imported EPUB, TXT, comic, and audio files, reading progress, bookmarks, annotations, bookshelf data, RSS subscriptions, book sources, replacement rules, and preferences are primarily stored locally. You can delete related data through iOS or in-app controls.

## Optional Sync and Sign-In

If you enable iCloud, WebDAV, or another sync feature, data is sent to the service you choose. iCloud is provided by Apple; WebDAV and other services are configured by you. We do not operate these services and do not receive their synced content.

If you use Apple or Google sign-in, the app stores basic account details locally, such as display name, email, and account identifier, to identify your account and sync state. Sign-in is not required for the reading features.

## Network Sources You Configure

Yuedu Reader can connect to RSS, book source, online reading, TTS, or WebDAV URLs that you provide. Requests are sent to the third-party services you choose; their own policies govern their handling of data. Do not send sensitive content to sources you do not trust.

## Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page. Material changes may also be noted in the app or on the project page.
